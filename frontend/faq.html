Clear details on storage, Vedic processes, bulk ordering, and transit practices.

What is Bilona Desi Ghee?
▼
Bilona Desi Ghee is traditionally churned butter fat obtained from the curd of grass-fed Desi cow milk using a wooden churner (Bilona) in two directions. It is rich in nutrients and vitamins.
Why does the color and texture vary slightly?
▼
Since our cows graze freely on organic pastures throughout the year, their diet changes with seasonal crops (Ashwagandha, alfalfa, grasses). This causes minor, natural changes in color and grain sizing, verifying that our ghee is 100% organic and dye-free.
How should I store Adwait Desi Ghee?
▼
Store Adwait Desi Ghee in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight. Do not refrigerate, as cold temperatures destroy the granular texture and degrade aromatic lipids. Always use a clean, dry spoon to avoid moisture contamination.
What is the shelf life of Adwait Desi Ghee?
▼
Due to our traditional cooking process, our ghee is highly stable and has a shelf life of 6 to 8 months at room temperature.
