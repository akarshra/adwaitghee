TRADITIONAL BILONA METHOD

The Vedic Clay-Pot Cycle

Step 1: Curd Culturing

Raw milk from Desi cows is boiled and set into curd in large earthen clay pots overnight.

Step 2: Curd Churning

The whole curd is churned clockwise and counter-clockwise with a wooden Bilona staff.

Step 3: Slow Heating

Separated butter (Makkhan) is boiled slowly on fire using cow-dung patties as heating source.
